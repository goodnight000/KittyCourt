2. Information We Collect

  • Memories: Photos, captions, and reactions shared with your partner
  • AI Insights: Feedback on insights
  • Support Communications: Messages sent to customer support

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Features accessed, time spent in app, interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, app version, identifiers
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, diagnostics
  • Push Tokens: Notification tokens used to deliver reminders and updates

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Providers: If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information
  • Payment Platforms: Apple App Store and Google Play handle payments; we receive your subscription status but not payment details
SENSITIVE DATA NOTICE: We process information that may reveal relationship dynamics, emotional wellbeing, or intimate life details. This data can be considered sensitive under certain privacy laws (including Washington's My Health My Data Act and GDPR special category data). We treat it with heightened protection and use it only to provide the App and features you request.

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Your Partner

When you connect with a partner in the App, they can see:

  • Your profile information (name, avatar, bio, preferences)
  • Court cases you participate in together
  • Appreciations you send to them
  • Your responses to daily questions
  • Shared calendar events and memories

When You Disconnect:

  • Your partner will no longer see future content you create
  • Historical shared content may remain visible to both parties unless removed or accounts are deleted
  • We cannot control screenshots or exports your partner may have made while connected

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

4.4 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access and least privilege
  • Authentication: Secure authentication and row-level access controls
  • Monitoring: Monitoring for unauthorized access and abuse

No system is perfectly secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

6.1 Active Account Data

We retain your personal data while your account is active to provide the App.

6.2 Deleted Account Data

Deleting your account is the only way to fully delete your personal data. When you delete your account:

  • In most cases, we delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days of account deletion
  • Backups and limited operational logs may persist for a limited period for recovery, security, fraud prevention, or legal obligations
  • If you are connected to a partner, you must disconnect before account deletion can be completed
  • Content shared with a partner may remain visible to that partner unless they also delete their account

6.3 De-identified Data

We may retain de-identified or aggregated data for analytics and product improvement, including Pause Wrapped summaries. This data cannot be used to identify you.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data is processed and stored in Canada and the United States, and may be transferred to other locations where our service providers operate. If you access the App from outside these regions, your data will be transferred internationally.

Where required, we rely on standard contractual clauses or other lawful transfer mechanisms.

8. Your Privacy Rights

8.1 General Rights (All Users)

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information in your profile
  • Deletion: Delete your account to remove your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

8.2 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have additional rights including the right to know, delete, and correct personal information. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

8.3 Washington Residents (My Health My Data Act)

If you are a Washington State resident ("WA user"), your relationship and emotional wellbeing data may qualify as consumer health data under Washington's My Health My Data Act. Subject to applicable law, you may request confirmation of collection, access, deletion, and withdrawal of consent for certain processing and sharing. We do not sell this data.

If we deny your request, you may appeal by emailing support@midnightstudio.app with the subject line "WA Privacy Appeal".

8.4 EEA/UK Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA or UK, you have rights under GDPR, including access, rectification, deletion, restriction, portability, objection, and withdrawal of consent. We process sensitive data based on explicit consent and contractual necessity.

8.5 Other Jurisdictions

If you are in another jurisdiction with applicable privacy laws, you may have similar rights. Contact us at support@midnightstudio.app to learn more or exercise your rights.

8.6 How to Submit Requests

To submit a privacy request, email support@midnightstudio.app from your account email and include enough detail for us to locate your account and process your request. We may request additional information to verify your identity before completing your request.

Where required by law, we respond within 45 days and may extend once by an additional 45 days with notice. If your request is denied, you may appeal through the same email address.
