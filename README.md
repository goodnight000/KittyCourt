Pause is a relationship wellness app for couples who want a calmer, kinder way to work through disagreements. It blends playful cat-themed design with AI-guided mediation, daily connection rituals, and shared tools that make it easier to stay close, even when you clash.

## What it does

- **The Court of Judge Whiskers**: Submit a disagreement and get an impartial, empathetic verdict that validates both sides and suggests a constructive resolution.
- **Case history**: Revisit past cases to see what worked and how you've grown together.
- **Daily Meow**: Answer a new question each day to spark meaningful (or silly) conversations.
- **Mood check-ins**: Track how you feel with expressive cat avatars and spot patterns over time.
- **Shared calendar**: Plan dates, remember anniversaries, and coordinate life in one place.
- **Kibble rewards**: Earn and spend in-app rewards that turn good communication into real-life perks.

## Who it's for

Couples who want a lighter, more supportive way to resolve conflict, build intimacy, and keep communication fun and consistent.
